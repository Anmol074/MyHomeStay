# About Giddha Pahar Homestay

Welcome to **Giddha Pahar Homestay**, a personal sanctuary nestled in the beautiful hills of Kurseong, right by St. Anthony's School.

## Our Story

Giddha Pahar is more than just a place to stay—it's a refuge from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Perched in the serene Himalayan foothills, our homestay offers guests an authentic experience of hill living with impeccable sunrises and the magical misty moods that the mountains are famous for.

## What Awaits You

- **Breathtaking Sunrises**: Wake up to stunning mountain vistas and golden hour beauty
- **Tranquil Atmosphere**: Experience the peaceful ambiance of the hills
- **Authentic Experience**: Immerse yourself in local culture and hospitality
- **Perfect Location**: Situated conveniently near St. Anthony's School in Kurseong

## Why Choose Us?

Our homestay is designed for travelers seeking an escape to nature, a retreat for reflection, or a base for exploring the picturesque Darjeeling district. Whether you're looking for adventure or relaxation, Giddha Pahar welcomes you with open arms.
